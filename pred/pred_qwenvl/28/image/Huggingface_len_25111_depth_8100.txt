A golden retriever with a glossy coat looks like a golden retriever, which is a medium to large-sized breed of dog. They have a thick, water-repellent coat that can range in color from light to dark golden. The coat is usually wavy or curly and can be long or short depending on the coat type. The golden retriever has a muscular, athletic build with a strong, powerful frame. They have a friendly, intelligent expression with large, expressive eyes and a kind, gentle demeanor. The golden retriever is known for its friendly, outgoing nature and is often used as a family pet or working dog.
