A golden retriever with a shiny coat looks like a golden retriever, which is a medium to large-sized breed of dog. They have a thick, water-repellent coat that comes in various shades of gold, from light blonde to dark golden. The coat is dense and wavy, giving it a soft and plush appearance. The golden retriever's coat is also known for its shine, which is due to the reflection of light off the individual hairs.
